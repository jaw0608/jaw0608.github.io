About me:

My name is Joseph and from a young age, my family considered me a tech-wizard. I was not (and still am not) a wizzard -I just knew how to restart the router. But for some reason, my family always asked me for help with technology. I'll skip the middle parts, but by 12 I was already replacing computer parts, making batch scripts, and, of course, port forwarding my Minecraft server.

Over time, I realized that I had the most fun making scripts, and began to learn Python. As if it were fate, a Computer Science course opened up the next year at my high school. Aaaaand the rest is history. I decided I wanted to go to school for Computer Science, and found my home at Stony Brook University!

Now, I hone in on my passion everyday! I have built a few projects at both Hackathons and my previous employment, and had the wonderful oppurtunity to teach my coding knowledge to children. I love everything about what I do, and I'm glad my family was inept at technology (although I did teach them how to reset the router themselves eventually...)
