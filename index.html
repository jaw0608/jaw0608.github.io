Joseph Wilson

Bio:

Joseph Wilson is a Computer Science Student at Stony Brook University who works with educators to spread knowledge using technology. Joe believes that technology is a useful informational tool and hopes to use his knowledge in software to teach, inform, and connect society. After being a childcare counselor, Joe moved on to teach children programming skills and techniques to children at theCoderSchool. Continuing the educational adventure at Stony Brook, Joe developed the brain-mapping software BrainPicker for Neuroscience students at his university and took on the role of Treasurer in the Stony Brook Computing Society. Joe continues to use technology to connect different fields, having worked as a technology consultant for a childcare nonforprofit and a local Jeweler business. Joe is currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science and is expected to graduate in 2021.
